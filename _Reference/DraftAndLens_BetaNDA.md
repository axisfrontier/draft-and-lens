# Draft & Lens — Beta Tester Agreement & Mutual NDA

> **Plain-English note (not part of the agreement):** This is a light, one-page agreement to set expectations with your private beta testers and give you a deterrent and legal recourse if someone copies or leaks the product. It is a starting template, not legal advice — if anything material is at stake, have a solicitor glance over it before use. Fill the bracketed fields, send it as a PDF, and ask each tester to reply with their name, the date, and the words "I agree" (a typed signature or e-signature is stronger if you can).

---

**BETA TESTING AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is made between:

**Discloser:** [Your full name / company name], of [your address or email], owner and operator of the software product known as **Draft & Lens** (the "Product"); and

**Tester:** [Tester's full name], of [tester's email] (the "Tester").

**Effective date:** [date]

The Discloser is granting the Tester early, pre-release access to the Product for evaluation and feedback. In exchange, the Tester agrees to the following.

---

**1. Confidential Information.**
"Confidential Information" means the Product and everything about how it works that is not public — including its interface and design, its analytical method and the way it reads and assesses written work, the wording and behaviour of its editorial and "lens" perspectives, its prompts and underlying logic, its pricing plans, its roadmap, and any other non-public information the Tester sees through their access. It does not include information the Tester can show was already public, or already known to them, through no fault of their own.

**2. The Tester agrees to:**
(a) keep the Confidential Information confidential and not share, publish, post, screenshot for distribution, or describe it in public (including on social media, forums, or to the press) without the Discloser's written permission;
(b) not copy, reproduce, reverse-engineer, scrape, extract, or attempt to recreate any part of the Product, its prompts, its lens perspectives, or its method, whether for themselves or anyone else;
(c) not use the Product or anything learned from it to build, train, or assist a competing product or service;
(d) use the access only for the agreed purpose — evaluating the Product and giving the Discloser honest feedback — and not for commercial exploitation;
(e) not share their access or login with anyone else.

**3. The Tester's own work stays the Tester's own.**
Nothing in this Agreement gives the Discloser any ownership of the scripts, stories, or other writing the Tester submits to the Product. That work remains entirely the Tester's. The Discloser will handle submitted work in line with its stated privacy practices and will not use it to train any model.

**4. Feedback.**
Any feedback, suggestions, or ideas the Tester gives about the Product may be used by the Discloser freely, with no obligation or payment, to improve the Product. The Tester is not entitled to any compensation, credit, or stake in the Product in exchange for testing or feedback (unless the Discloser agrees otherwise in writing).

**5. No warranty.**
The Product is pre-release and provided "as is." It may contain errors, give imperfect analysis, or change or break without notice. The Discloser makes no guarantees about it and is not liable for any loss arising from the Tester's use of it, to the fullest extent the law allows.

**6. Access can end at any time.**
The Discloser may withdraw the Tester's access at any time, for any reason, without notice.

**7. How long this lasts.**
The Tester's confidentiality obligations continue for **two (2) years** from the effective date, or until the relevant information becomes public through no fault of the Tester — whichever comes first.

**8. General.**
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [England and Wales / your jurisdiction]. It is the whole agreement between the parties about the beta. If any part is found unenforceable, the rest still applies.
